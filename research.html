Cutting-Edge Research

Driving discovery and innovation at the frontiers of synthetic biology.

Our Research Pillars

Genomic Engineering for Enhanced Functionality

Our research focuses on precision genomic engineering to unlock and enhance specific biological functions. We utilize advanced CRISPR-based systems and proprietary gene editing technologies to modify organisms with unparalleled accuracy and efficiency.

This work is critical for developing next-generation microbial cell factories, therapeutic agents, and bioremediation solutions, pushing the boundaries of what engineered biology can achieve.

Advanced Biomanufacturing

Sustainable Production through Synthetic Pathways

We are engineering novel metabolic pathways in various host organisms to produce high-value chemicals, fuels, and materials sustainably. Our research aims to reduce reliance on petrochemicals and establish a greener manufacturing future.

This includes optimizing enzyme cascades, developing robust chassis organisms, and implementing advanced bioprocess engineering to maximize yield and purity, paving the way for industrial-scale bio-production.

Data-Driven Discovery

AI and Machine Learning in Biology

Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning, we are accelerating the discovery and design of biological systems. Our computational approaches predict genetic interactions, optimize metabolic fluxes, and guide experimental design.

This integration of cutting-edge informatics with high-throughput experimentation allows us to navigate complex biological design spaces more effectively, leading to faster breakthroughs and more predictable outcomes.
