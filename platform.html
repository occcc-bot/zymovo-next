The Zymovo Platform

An advanced ecosystem for engineered biology, empowering discovery and production.

Precision Engineering at Scale

Integrated Design-Build-Test-Learn Cycle

Our proprietary platform integrates computational design, automated high-throughput biology, and advanced data analytics to accelerate the development of novel biological systems. We streamline the entire engineering workflow, from gene synthesis to functional validation.

This closed-loop system allows for rapid iteration and optimization, enabling us to efficiently engineer microbes, enzymes, and pathways for a wide range of applications, ensuring unparalleled precision and performance.

Modular Bio-Components

Building Blocks for Breakthroughs

The Zymovo platform is built upon a foundation of well-characterized and highly functional modular bio-components. This library of standardized genetic parts, enzymes, and metabolic pathways allows for unprecedented flexibility and predictability in biological engineering.

By leveraging these optimized building blocks, our scientists can rapidly prototype and deploy complex biological functions, drastically reducing development timelines and increasing the success rate of new bioproduction ventures.

Applications & Capabilities

Driving Innovation Across Industries

Our platform's versatility extends across various sectors, including sustainable chemistry, advanced materials, and next-generation therapeutics. We partner with innovators to transform challenges into opportunities, providing bespoke biological solutions.

Whether it's enhancing enzyme efficiency, engineering microbial factories for novel compounds, or developing biosensors for environmental monitoring, Zymovo is equipped to deliver cutting-edge biotechnological advancements.
